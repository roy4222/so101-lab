User is building a robotics learning project based on HuggingFace LeRobot SO-101.
**Why:** Wants to learn robot arm knowledge and applications.
**How to apply:** Guide through LeRobot setup, training pipelines, and progressively complex tasks. Leverage their powerful multi-GPU workstation for training and Jetson for edge inference.

**Current state:** Two arms — one as leader (joystick), one as follower arm.
**Ultimate goal:** Evolve into XLeRobot (github.com/Vector-Wangel/XLeRobot) — dual-arm mobile robot on a cart with wheels. User plans to convert both to actuated arms and mount on a mobile base.
**Simulation interest:** NVIDIA Omniverse / Isaac Sim for sim-to-real transfer.
**Reference guide:** Embodied-AI-Guide (TianxingChen) for learning path.
